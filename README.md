# 🚀 HireX — Premium AI Career Suite & Job Application Tracker

HireX is an enterprise-grade SaaS platform built for modern job seekers to bypass traditional application systems. Instead of clicking "Easy Apply" and falling into black holes, HireX empowers candidates with direct cold-outreach automation, resume tailoring, and simulation tools.

🌐 **Live Demo:** [https://hirex-9g9p.onrender.com](https://hirex-9g9p.onrender.com)

---

## 🌟 Elite Features

### 1. 🎙️ Live AI Voice Interview (Web Speech API)
Simulate high-pressure interviews with native browser voice synthesizers. The AI reads role-specific questions and evaluates your spoken answers, giving you instant confidence scoring and tips using the STAR method.

### 2. ✨ 1-Click Resume Tailor & ATS Scanner
- **ATS Match Score:** A brutal, literal bot scores your resume against any Job Description, highlighting formatting errors and missing keywords.
- **1-Click Tailor:** Instantly rewrites your master resume's bullet points and summary to target the JD keywords automatically.

### 3. 🧠 Referral Hacker & Cold Email Pitcher
- **recruiter.email@company.com:** Automatically guesses recruiter email formats based on company domains.
- **Pain Point Pitch:** Generates a short, hyper-personalized outreach draft focused on solving the team's business problems rather than asking for a job.
- **1-Click Send:** Launches your native mail client with all details pre-filled.

### 4. 🌐 Shareable Public Career Card
Publish a premium personal brand page to a public URL (e.g., `/u/satyamsharma`) to share directly on LinkedIn or Twitter, featuring cold introduction pitches and auto-optimized summaries.

### 5. 🔖 Save-from-Web Bookmarklet
A drag-and-drop browser bookmarklet that clips job details from LinkedIn, Indeed, etc., and adds them to your tracker in one click without leaving the page.

### 6. 📊 Pipeline Analytics & PDF Export
Visualize your application velocity (Applied, Interviewing, Offered) using interactive charts and export clean progress reports.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js, Vite, Vanilla CSS, Recharts, Lucide Icons
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB (Mongoose)
- **AI Engine:** Google Gemini Pro / 2.5-Flash (Integrated with robust static failover)
